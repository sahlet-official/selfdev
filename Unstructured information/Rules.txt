Rules gained on experience and which are very recommended to follow:

1. If you don't want to - don't do it.

2. There is always option to do nothing.

3. If you want to do something - you always have the choice to do it or not.

4. If you already doing something - don't forget to ask yourself sometimes if you still want it or not.

5. Is there some sense in life? No. It just exists.

6. Why do you live? Nowhy. There is no reason. Just make choice to live or no - it is up to you.

7. Why people make suicide? Mostly because they suffer.

8. Happiness or suffer are optional and depends on your choice.

9. How to learn to be happy? Start with non-resistance (acceptance) of suffering and you will see how it decrease.
